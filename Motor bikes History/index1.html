The first motorbike

The first gasoline-powered motorcycle, the Daimler Reitwagen, was created in 1885 by Gottlieb Daimler and Wilhelm Maybach in Germany. This invention marked the beginning of motorcycle history, evolving from earlier steam-powered experiments and paving the way for mass production and diverse motorcycle types.
